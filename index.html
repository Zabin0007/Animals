Animal Matters TO me (ANMM) is a clinical rescue facility for strays & abandoned ownerless animals, birds & reptiles in need of help. We run a shelter cum hospital in Malad, Mumbai and offer services of Adoption, Animal Shelter, Medical Treatment, Sterilisation & Animal Ambulance
